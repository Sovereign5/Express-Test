Welcome to Mercury!


The smallest and innermost planet in the solar system, Mercury, named after the Roman messanger god, revolves around the sun in a tidally locked orbit. Mercury is also one of the four terrestrial planets in the solar system. The planet's density signifies that it has an iron-rich core.
